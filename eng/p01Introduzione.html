to Tap

The tap is indeed one of the most common and useful devices in our homes, giving us water whenever we need it and in whatever amount. Although it is a familiar object, few people know why it is actually called a tap. Here are three possible answers, only one of which, is correct. It's up to you to guess which… The word comes from

1. Tappen, a town in North Dakota, in the United States, where the first factory making taps was established in the 19th century;
2. Tappô, a proto-germanic term referring to the tapered cylindrical peg used to stop the flow in a casket or barrel;

3. Karl Heinz Von Tappen, a German physicist who, in the 16th century, invented a mechanism which controlled the flow of water in houses.
