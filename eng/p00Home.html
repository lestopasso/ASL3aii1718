Sustainable development can be defined as the organising principle which allows everyone to live on this planet without destroying the natural systems and without exceeding their ability to support the emissions, debris and waste caused by our production activities.
This experience, through games, fun riddles and word puzzles, will take you on a journey which shows how water, energy and waste can be managed in a correct way also following simple rules for responsible use and consumption.
