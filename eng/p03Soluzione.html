Blue Gold

Water is indispensable for our survival and its presence is strongly linked to the well-being of our society. A percentage to keep in mind is 0.065% which represents the quantity of available water son our planet. The rest is found in the sea, in the form of ice or in inaccessible areas. And remember that in many parts of the world, there is a shortage of water, which can even become a cause of conflict. In addition to this, there is pollution, which can contaminate fresh water reserves... It is, therefore, of the utmost necessity to preserve this precious asset. For example, all water utility companies must regularly repair or prevent leakage by means of careful maintenance and by treating the resources so as to safeguard our health and the environment.
But there are things that you can do too...

  • For instance, you can: avoid leaving the tap running when brushing your teeth;

  • use a tap aerator to reduce the flow;

  • timely repair leaks in your home;

  • wash fruit and vegetables by leaving them to soak and not by washing them under running water;

  • use the washing machine and the dish washer only when they are fully loaded;

  • and avoid throwing toxic products into the sink or down the toilet.

These are small, everyday actions, which contribute to conserving and protecting our precious blue gold.
