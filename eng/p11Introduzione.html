Where does It
Come from?

The word energy comes from the Greek word "ἐνέργεια" (enérgheia) and it means living force. Energy is like a chameleon: there are various forms (for example, chemical, thermal and electric energy) and each tends to change from one form to another. We find it in the sun, in fossil fuels, and in the movement of water. Do you know what energy form we should use from a sustainable development point of view?
